Silicone Rubber Products

View Online Catalog

Reflect Scientific offers a variety of silicone based products that can be used across a very broad range of industrial applications.


Silicone compounds are perhaps the most versatile materials on the market today. They can outperform all other elastomers in a temperature range of -65 to 500 °F.


No matter how diverse your needs are, we can provide an exacting product! If you'd like to find out more about Silicone Rubber Products, and more about what the Reflect Scientific team can do for your specific project, call 801-226-4100.

Typically they find application in the following areas:

  • Sealants/Septas
  • Coatings
  • Lubricants
  • Adhesives
  • Conductivity media

Formed sheets, membranes and thin films formed products are available in cut sizes, sheets and pads with the following options:

  • Teflon, Mylar or Aluminum lined
  • Cross slit
  • Laser drilled holes
  • GC injection port septa
  • Vial cap liners
